the dog is a 1st class citizen

in towns predominated by

yuppies and dinkies

places of business set out water

and employees hand out treats

strangers genuinely enquire

about age, name and gender

and everyone smiles

as the dog walks by

wagging at them


whereas


the toddler is a 2nd class citizen

in towns predominated by

yuppies and dinkies

places of business may set out chairs

and employees hand out receipts

strangers haphazardly enquire

about age

and no one smiles

as the toddler walks by

waving at them
