dogs are 1st class citizens

in towns predominated by

yuppies and dinkies

places of business set out water

and employees hand out treats

strangers genuinely enquire

about age, name and gender

and everyone smiles

as they walk by

wagging


toddlers are 2nd class citizens

in towns predominated by

yuppies and dinkies

places of business may set out chairs

and employees always dish out receipts

strangers haphazardly enquire

about age (if at all)

and no one smiles

as they walk by

waving


no wonder then

that my toddler's sole objective

is to hit our dog

it is class warfare, after all

and he is losing badly

in our hometown

that's predominated by

yuppies and dinkies
