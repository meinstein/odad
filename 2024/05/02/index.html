my dog is a 1st class citizen

in towns predominated by

yuppies and dinkies

places of business set out water

and employees hand out treats

strangers genuinely enquire

about age, name and gender

and everyone smiles

as he walks by

wagging


my toddler is a 2nd class citizen

in towns predominated by

yuppies and dinkies

places of business may set out chairs

and employees always dish out receipts

strangers haphazardly enquire

about age, if at all

and no one smiles

as he walks by

waving


little wonder then

that my toddler's main objective

is to hit the dog

it's class warfare, after all

and he's losing badly

in towns predominated by

yuppies and dinkies
